WHAT IS RUMBA ?


The termrumba was originally a generic word for other terms. For example, it is related to words for Son, Danzon, Guaracha representing music and dancing from the West Indies. In addition, it may have evolved from other words like rumbear to party or rumbo, which means course or party. Also, the word may be related to rhombus meaning something that spins. Moreover, the verbs rumbaing and rumbaed were first recorded in 1932 (according to the Online Etymology Dictionary ).
