WHAT IS FLAMENCO DANCING ?


Flamenco is the traditional song and dance of the Gypsies (flamencos) of Andalucia. Its evolution is a source of great debate with no accepted explanation of how flamenco resulted from the cultural influences of such diverse regions as North Africa, Southern Europe and the Near and Far East.
